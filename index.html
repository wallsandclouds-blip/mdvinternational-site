What We Do

Equity strategies built around process, rotation, and risk management.

MDV International develops equity-based investment strategies and portfolio solutions for funds, qualified investors, and institutional partners.

Equity Strategy Development

We design and structure investment strategies for disciplined exposure to public equity markets, grounded in research, market experience, and defined objectives.

Systematic Stock Selection

We use a structured process to identify high-quality stocks based on fundamentals, factor analysis, market leadership, and relative strength.

Portfolio Rotation

Our system is designed to help rotate exposure as conditions change, seeking to remain aligned with areas of strength while reducing exposure to weaker opportunities.

Options Hedging

We use options as a risk management tool to help protect portfolios against downside risk, manage volatility, and improve portfolio resilience.

Our Investment Approach

Equity Market Leadership

Clear Decision-Making System

Risk Management

Risk management is central to our process. We seek to define, measure, and manage downside exposure while maintaining flexibility to adapt as conditions change.

Capital Preservation

Protecting capital is essential to long-term compounding. Our approach is designed to pursue return opportunities while avoiding unnecessary complexity and uncontrolled risk.

Leadership

Led by Marcello Vieira

MDV International is led by Marcello Vieira, a former physician turned full-time investor with over 20 years of experience in financial markets.

Marcello began investing during medical school and later transitioned from a successful medical career to become a full-time investor.

Over the years, he has developed a disciplined investment approach focused on equity strategies, portfolio construction, systematic decision-making, risk management, and the strategic use of options for hedging.

His background combines analytical thinking, independent research, and decades of real market experience. Today, his work is focused on building and applying investment strategies for investors who value discipline, structure, and risk control.
